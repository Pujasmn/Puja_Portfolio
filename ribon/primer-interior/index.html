INTERIO(interior)

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Interio Interior Primer is specially formulated & based on acrylic co-polymer & It can be used in interior only as priming coat for both new & old surfaces of ploster before application of finish coat.

SURFACE PREPARATION

    PLASTER

New masonry surfaces must be allowed to cure completely. It is recomended to allow 28 days as the curing time for new masonary surfaces.

    CLEANING

Ensure the surface is completely free from all dirt, loosely held plaster, powdery residue, oil grease or any other contamination. Any previous growth of fungus, algae or most needs to be removed thoroughly by vigorous wire brushing and cleaning with water.

    FILLING THE CRACKS, HOLES & DENTS

For filling cracks upto 3mm, holes and dents use RIBON Wall Putty or white cement and fine sand in the ratio 1:3 for Interior surfaces or substrates that are subjected to continuous seepage or dampness, kindly get the repairs done before the application of point.

WHITE Interio Interior Primer is an undercoat

Dilute one litre of Vibe Interior Primer with 500 ml of clean potable water. Do not add any stainer/ colorant to the Interio Interior Primer. Don't over dilute Interio Interior Primer.

Varles according to the porosity and texture of the surface. Coverage for smooth surface with no suction: Approx 85-100 Sq. ft. per litre per coat.

The pack sizes available for Interio Interior Primer are 20 ltr., 10 ltr., 4ltr. and 1 ltr.

Keep containers airtight during handling and storage to avoid lass of material. Store Interio Interior Primer in a cool dry place and also protect it from extreme climatic conditions during storage, in transit and at site.

Its not advisable to use any waterproofing chemical os base, top coot or additive. Over dilution of Interio interior Primer may result in poor hiding, poor banding or flaking. Excessive brushing and stretching are also to be avoided. No thinner or universal stainer is to be used with interio Interior Primer.
