ELITE(exterior)

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Ribon paints Exterior Prism Emulsion Glow is a modified ocrylic emulsion based exterior point suitable for moderate to dry climatic regions. The paint has higher sheen compared to economy paints, therefore imports rich look on the exterior walls. The product is resistant to chalking, flaking, fading as well as prevents the growth of algae and fungi.

SURFACE PREPARATION

    PLASTER

For New masonry surfaces must be allowed to cure completely. The surface should be allowed to cure completely. Apply one coat of Illusion Sealer to avoid salt petering.

    CLEANING

Ensure the surface is free from all loose paints, dust particles, chalk, grease, fungus and algae. Sand the surface with Emery Paper No. 80 and wipe or scrub it clean by wire brushing.

    FILLING THE CRACKS, HOLES & DENTS

For filling cracks upto 3mm, holes and dents use Ribon Wall Putty or white cement and fine sand in the ratio 1:3 For Exterior surfaces or substrates that are subjected to continuous seepage or dampness, kindly get the repairs done before the top coat application.

  • • Strong UV Resistance
  • • 4 to 5 Years Of Life

Mix 2.5 Parts of ELITE EXTERIOR EMULSION PAINT with 1 part of clean potable water by volume for better brushability.

Coverage is 50-60 Sq. ft. per liter for 2 coat. Actual coverage may vary according to the porosity and texture of the surface. For abundant precaution, an initial trial may be undertaken to determine covering capacity on very rough or abnormal surfaces.

The pack sizes available for Ribon Elite Exterior Emulsion are 20 ltr., 10 ltr., 4ltr. and 1 ltr.
